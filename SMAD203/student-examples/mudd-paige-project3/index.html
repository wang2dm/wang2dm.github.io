about me

Hi there! I'm Paige, a sophomore at James Madison University. I am a double major in Media Arts & Design (SMAD) and Theatre Design. I have experience with visual design for the web and stage. Some things I enjoy studying are history, literature, music, communications, machinery, electronics, and how things work. I lovee bullet journaling, drawing, spending time with friends, editing videos, and talking to people. My favorite thing right now is finding a good place outside to read or draw.

& my work

As a designer, I challenge myself to be versatile in what I create for others. I have experience with designing for print, screen, and the web. I have film experience, both in the production phase and the post-production aspects, with over seven years of editing experience under my belt. In the past, I designed the website for Dent Removers RVA and designed logos for Hannah's Bracelets and Divine Sacred Gifts. Currently, I serve as the digital editor for scratch pad, the student ad agency at James Madison University, and I am a member of the Theatre and Dance social media team for James Madison University, with an emphasis on task management and content creation. My organizational skills and task-driven nature help the social media team run smoothly and ensures that we are staying on track.

Looking to the future, I want to make fun, effective designs that tell a story and make a positive impact on the world. As a positive person myself, my art should reflect the type of things I want to see in the world.

portfolio
Self Reflection

The nature of this Adobe Photoshop project was about finding yourself in a series of words picked by the class. With the help of surveys, each class member could only choose five words that were completely different from their peers. We moved into photoshop, focusing on how the words can be seen without text in the project.

'A Public Place'

This scenic design was created for Shakespeare's Taming of the Shrew, where Petruchio and Katharina fight. It was submitted to the Virginia Theatre Association's Annual Scenic Design competition in 2019 and received second place overall.

'Data Visualization'

These are three different roughs for a data visualization for World Energy Consumption by Source, created using Adobe Illustrator. The data is visualized in a way that is easy to read and clean to look at. The final submission was a variation of the bottom right.

'Virginia House White Model'

A white model of the Drawing Room in Virginia House, located in Richmond, Virginia. The room was based on photos of the room and was part of a group study on period rooms. The model is completely to scale and is as accurate as possible.

'Hannah's Bracelets' Logo

A logo for local Richmond-based bracelet shop, Hannah's Bracelets. It was later used for print in business cards and a blank one was used for Story Highlights on Instagram.

'Candle Puzzle' Props

The candles were carved for Escape Room RVA's room, "Algernon's Shop of Magical Goods". The symbols were chosen, carved into them, and used a mosaic-esque style of painting, where the paint was put on and wiped away.
